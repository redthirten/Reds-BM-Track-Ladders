[h1]Features:[/h1]
[list]
    [*]Attaches the track ladder rungs along the length of the closest track spine automatically
    [*]Can be attached to any side of the spine (top, bottom, left, or right)
    [*]The number of rungs, their spacing, and direction of travel can be configured with entity parameters.
    [*]Re-colorable
    [*]Lightweight, with ladder rungs that are low-poly and de-spawn when not close to the camera to save performance
[/list]

[hr][/hr]
[h1]How to Extract & Use:[/h1]
[list]
    [*]Open the park in your editor.
    [*]Click on File > Extract Package, and choose where you want to save the park.
    [*]Open the park in your File Explorer. Then open the scenery folder.
    [*]Copy the reds_bm_track_ladders folder to any park of your own that you want to use it with.
    [*]Open your personal park in the editor.
    [*]Select a ladder of your choosing and place the node next to the start (or end) of a desired track section.
    [*]Double click the node for configuration settings. The ladder will appear along the spine of the track in Play mode.
[/list]

[hr][/hr]
[h1]Known Issues:[/h1]
Unfortunately, the ladders only accommodate standard size B&M track spines at this time due to complex relative-positioning issues. Therefore, the ladders may not work properly with the following:
[list]
    [*]Non-B&M coaster styles
    [*]Dive Coaster styles
    [*]Medium or Large Box spine types
[/list]
